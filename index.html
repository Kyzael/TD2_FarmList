⚠ Important notes before you farm

  • Targeted loot is king. Set it to the desired gear slot in Countdown (Heroic+) or The Summit — named enemies all drop that slot type. Switch targets freely after each drop.
  • Eagle Bearer is raid-locked. It only drops from the Operation Dark Hours final chest (weekly reset) or rare DZ extracts. Targeted loot does NOT work for it.
  • Pestilence requires the Dark Zone. It drops from named DZ bosses only. Extract contaminated loot. It enters the general loot pool after your first drop.
  • Chatterbox must be crafted first. Farm Hyena Faction Crates across DC for the three components and blueprint (Bank HQ mission vault). After crafting, it joins the normal exotic pool.
  • St Elmo's Engine is shared across Striker, AR, and SHD builds — getting a god roll early has outsized value. It's a very common drop from AR targeted loot in Countdown.
  • SHD/XP: stack directives. Each directive adds +25% XP. During 5× XP events, loop Classified Assignment missions for 100–130 SHD levels/hour. Outside events, Heroic Control Points + 4-5 directives is the meta.
  • Tinkering Station is everywhere. Press M anywhere to open it. Recalibrate talents and attributes, optimize item level, then raise Proficiency/Expertise.
  • Exotic Caches. Completing Countdown runs earns Countdown Requisition — spend it on Exotic Caches to fill gaps without farming a specific slot.

Build reference — PvE & XP farm builds 2026

⌄
PVE Builds
1. Striker + Overdogs/Equalizer Gunner
The fastest Striker stack variant — Overdogs gloves synergise with stacks for enormous sustained crit damage. Primary choice for all Heroic content and the go-to DPS meta in 2026.
Strength: Fastest stack building, top-tier sustained DPS Weakness: Needs sustained fire — stacks drop fast if you stop shooting
2. St Elmo's Striker + Memento Gunner
The Legendary specialist — Memento's Kill Confirmed stacks amplify Striker damage to absurd levels. Best build for Legendary Strongholds and Summit when you can maintain aggressive kills.
Strength: Massive snowball damage — gets stronger with every kill Weakness: Must pick up trophies from kills, forcing aggressive positioning
3. Heartbreaker PvE Technician
The tanky DPS build — Heartbreaker's blue cores give survivability while Flatline + Linked Laser Pointer deliver incredible damage. Excellent for Countdown, Summit, and group Heroic/Legendary content.
Strength: High damage AND survivability — hard to die with blue cores Weakness: Needs shotgun swap to build stacks, clunky weapon cycling
4. Assault Rifle PvE Gunner
The flexible all-rounder — brand-mix build using best-in-slot named items instead of gear sets. Easier to gear than set builds and still extremely potent for all Heroic PvE content.
Strength: Easy to farm — no set bonus dependency, swap pieces freely Weakness: Lower damage ceiling than full set builds at max optimization
5. Kingbreaker Spotter Technician
One of the strongest builds in the game — the triple synergy between Spotter, Flatline, and Heartbreaker stacks creates exceptional damage with blue-core survivability. A top pick for all end-game PvE.
Strength: Triple talent synergy — incredible damage + tanky blue cores Weakness: Requires Technician's Linked Laser Pointer and a specific exotic AR
6. Pestilence LMG Gunner
The plague spreader — enemies dying while infected spread damage to nearby targets, creating chain reactions across entire groups. A versatile brand-mix DPS build for Heroic and group content.
Strength: Plague spreading devastates clustered enemies, great AoE Weakness: First Pestilence drop requires Dark Zone farming
7. Negotiator's Pestilence Gunner
The chain reaction build — Negotiator's marks + Pestilence plague = explosive chain kills. Best for Legendary content where enemies cluster tightly. One of the top DPS builds for coordinated group play.
Strength: Chain explosions wipe entire groups — dominant in Legendary Weakness: Glass cannon with all red cores, requires careful cover play
8. SMG Build for PvE Gunner
The fastest clear-speed build — Hunter's Fury rewards rapid close-range kills while Chatterbox provides near-infinite ammo. Best speed farm for Heroic Control Points, Missions, and Summit.
Strength: Insane close-range DPS, near-infinite ammo, fastest clears Weakness: Close range only — very fragile at distance or vs. snipers
9. Headhunter Regulus Pistol Technician
The one-shot pistol build — Regulus exotic pistol with Perfect Headhunter creates devastating headshot chains. Memento stacks amplify damage further. Clears even Legendary content in the right hands.
Strength: One-shot headshot kills, massive burst damage with Headhunter Weakness: Regulus is raid-exclusive (Iron Horse) — hardest exotic to obtain
XP / SHD Farming Builds
+ Hotshot Red (XP Farm) Sharpshooter
The all-red XP grinder — one-shots enemies from any range with maximum headshot damage. From the 3rd kill onward, Determined makes ammo free. Best for Heroic CPs during XP events.
Strength: Maximum headshot damage — fastest one-shot kills in the game Weakness: Pure glass cannon — zero survivability, miss and you're dead
+ Hotshot Hybrid (XP Farm) Gunner / Sharpshooter
The tanky XP variant — trades some headshot damage for blue cores and Bloodsucker survivability. Still one-shots most Heroic enemies while being much harder to kill.
Strength: Headshot one-shots + blue core survivability, safer than Red Weakness: Lower headshot damage ceiling than the all-red variant
+ HF Determined (XP Farm) Sharpshooter
Close-range headshot XP build — Hunter's Fury rewards rapid kills while Determined refunds ammo. Memento stacks make each successive kill hit harder. Aggressive CQC playstyle for fast clears.
Strength: Close-range headshot machine — Memento snowball + free ammo Weakness: Must land headshots at close range — high skill floor
+ Eclipse Status (XP Farm) Firewall
The status effect XP build — Eclipse Protocol spreads fire and status effects across entire enemy groups. Vile Mask adds tick damage. Cover-based, safe, and great for clearing Heroic CPs with directives.
Strength: Chain status effects wipe groups — very safe cover-based playstyle Weakness: Slow single-target DPS, weak against status-immune enemies
+ 1886 Determined (XP Farm) Sharpshooter
The balanced headhunter — 1886 rifle with Determined and Memento for sustained headshot kills. Habsburg/Palisade blue cores and PFE mods provide excellent survivability during extended XP sessions.
Strength: Best balance of damage + survivability for extended XP farming Weakness: Slower kill speed than pure headshot builds, needs rare pieces

Important notes before you farm

⌄
  • Targeted loot is king. Set it to the desired gear slot in Countdown (Heroic+) or The Summit — named enemies all drop that slot type. Switch targets freely after each drop.
  • Eagle Bearer is raid-locked. It only drops from the Operation Dark Hours final chest (weekly reset) or rare DZ extracts. Targeted loot does NOT work for it.
  • Regulus is raid-locked. It only drops from the Operation Iron Horse final boss (weekly reset). No targeted loot. Essential for the Headhunter Pistol build.
  • Pestilence requires the Dark Zone. It drops from named DZ bosses only. Extract contaminated loot. It enters the general loot pool after your first drop.
  • Chatterbox must be crafted first. Farm Hyena Faction Crates across DC for three components and the blueprint (Bank HQ mission vault). After crafting, it joins the normal exotic pool.
  • St Elmo's Engine is shared across Striker and AR builds — getting a god roll early has outsized value. It's a very common AR-targeted-loot drop in Countdown.
  • Nemesis must be crafted first. Farm four components from Invaded Strongholds (Capitol, Roosevelt, District Union Arena, Grand Washington Hotel) plus the blueprint from Tidal Basin. After crafting, it enters the normal exotic pool. Used across all XP farm builds.
  • SHD/XP: stack directives. Each directive adds +25% XP. During 5× XP events, loop Classified Assignments for 100–130 SHD levels/hour. Outside events, Heroic Control Points + 4-5 directives is the meta.
  • Tinkering Station is everywhere. Press M anywhere to open it. Recalibrate talents/attributes, optimize item level, then raise Proficiency/Expertise.
  • Exotic Caches. Completing Countdown earns Countdown Requisition — spend it on Exotic Caches to fill gaps without targeting a specific slot.
